rats should always have a solid, steady cage to stay in to protect them. rats are usually in their cages for 20+ hours every day, so they need a cage that keeps them secure and meets all of their needs. cages should be chew-proof, provide adequate ventilation, and filled with safe bedding and enough enrichment. there are a lot of good set-ups you can find online, but there are a lot of bad ones too!
